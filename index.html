14:00

Learn about the infamous 1692 witch trials through immersive exhibits.

19:00

Spooky walking tour through Salem's haunted streets with professional guides.

🗒️ Notes

Bring a jacket for the evening ghost tour - it gets chilly in October! Don't forget your camera for spooky photos.

🍂 Day 2: Boston Exploration

📍 Boston, Massachusetts
📅 Saturday, October 26, 2024
09:00

🚗 Drive to Boston

Scenic 45-minute drive through autumn foliage along Route 1A.

11:00

Walk the historic red-brick trail through downtown Boston, visiting 16 historical sites.

🗒️ Notes

Perfect time for leaf-peeping! The maples should be at peak color. Try some New England clam chowder for lunch.

📍 North Conway, New Hampshire
📅 Sunday, October 27, 2024
08:00

🚗 Drive to White Mountains

2.5 hour scenic drive through peak foliage via Route 16 North.

12:00

Historic train ride to the summit with spectacular 360-degree autumn views.

🗒️ Notes

Dress warmly - it's much colder at the summit! Bring layers and a warm hat.
